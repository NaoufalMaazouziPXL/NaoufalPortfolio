Follow my journey through wpl 1.

I worked on Workplace Learning 2 (WPL 2), which aims to create an environment that replicates real-world experiences for digital design. In WPL 2, participants are assigned to teams and tasked with creating a dummy company. The project involves various design and development activities, including designing and developing the website, designing the app, creating the brand book, designing the logo, and conducting interviews.

The goal of WPL 2 is to provide a hands-on learning experience where participants can apply their digital design skills in a practical setting. By working on these diverse tasks, participants gain valuable experience in creating a cohesive brand identity, developing digital assets, and engaging in interview processes. This immersive approach helps participants develop a well-rounded skill set and prepares them for real-world design challenges in the field of digital design and branding.
